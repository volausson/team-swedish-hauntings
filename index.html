About The Team

The team was founded in 2017 by Tom G Olausson, an author of horror stories and poetry. The purpose of TSH is first and foremost to capture proof of paranormal activity. These proofs of haunting is going to be the basis for the forthcoming book titled Swedish Hauntings, he is currently working on. As well as paranormal investigations, we also offer help with spiritual cleansing of your home.
